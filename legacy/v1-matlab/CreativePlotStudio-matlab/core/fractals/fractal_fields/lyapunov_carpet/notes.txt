Lyapunov Carpet
Domain: fractals | Category: Fractal Fields

Principle:
- Measures sensitivity of a logistic map whose parameter alternates spatially.
- Positive Lyapunov exponents indicate chaos; negative values indicate stability.
- The parameter plane becomes a carpet of stable and chaotic regions.

Formula / model:
λ = lim_N (1/N) Σ log |r_n(1-2x_n)|.
