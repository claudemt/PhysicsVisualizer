Gray-Scott Coral
Domain: fractals | Category: Fractal Fields

Principle:
- Two chemical species diffuse and react on a grid.
- Feed and kill rates control spots, worms, and coral-like textures.
- The pattern is a reaction-diffusion instability.

Formula / model:
u_t=D_u∇²u-uv²+F(1-u), v_t=D_v∇²v+uv²-(F+k)v.
