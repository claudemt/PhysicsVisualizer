Tricorn Mandelbar
Domain: fractals | Category: Escape-Time & Julia

Principle:
- The Mandelbar or Tricorn uses complex conjugation before squaring.
- Conjugation breaks analytic orientation and produces three-cornered symmetry.
- It is an anti-holomorphic counterpart of the Mandelbrot iteration.

Formula / model:
z_{n+1}=conj(z_n)^2+c.
