Celtic Mandelbrot
Domain: fractals | Category: Escape-Time & Julia

Principle:
- A folded Mandelbrot variant using the absolute value of the real part of z^2.
- The fold creates woven cusp shapes and mirror-like branches.
- It remains an escape-time fractal but no longer follows the standard holomorphic map.

Formula / model:
z_{n+1}=|Re(z_n^2)| + i Im(z_n^2) + c.
