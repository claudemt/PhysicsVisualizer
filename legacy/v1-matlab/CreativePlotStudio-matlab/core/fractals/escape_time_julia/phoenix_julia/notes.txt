Phoenix Julia
Domain: fractals | Category: Escape-Time & Julia

Principle:
- Adds memory of the previous iterate to a Julia-like recurrence.

- The parameter controlling z_{n-1} changes the swirl and branching.

Formula / model:
z_{n+1}=z_n^2+c+p z_{n-1}.
