Apollonian Gasket
Domain: fractals | Category: Recursive & IFS

Principle:
- Circle packing generated by repeatedly inserting circles tangent to three existing circles.
- Curvatures follow Descartes' circle theorem.
- The gap structure forms a fractal packing.

Formula / model:
(k1+k2+k3+k4)^2 = 2(k1^2+k2^2+k3^2+k4^2).
