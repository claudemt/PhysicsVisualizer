Levy C Curve
Domain: fractals | Category: Recursive & IFS

Principle:
- Each line segment is replaced by two perpendicular half-scale segments.
- The replacement rule corresponds to the sides of an isosceles right triangle.
- Repeated folding creates a self-similar C-shaped curve.

Formula / model:
Segment vector v is replaced by rotations of v/sqrt(2) by ±45°.
