Pythagoras Tree
Domain: fractals | Category: Recursive & IFS

Principle:
- Squares are recursively attached using a right-triangle construction.
- Child square side lengths follow the triangle legs.
- The visual tree encodes repeated Pythagorean decomposition.

Formula / model:
Parent square side c has child sides a,b satisfying a^2+b^2=c^2.
