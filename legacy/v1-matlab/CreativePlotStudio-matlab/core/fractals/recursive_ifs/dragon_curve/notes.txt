Dragon Curve
Domain: fractals | Category: Recursive & IFS

Principle:
- A recursive folding curve generated by repeated turns.
- The turn sequence can be built by reversing and complementing previous turns.
- The curve fills space increasingly densely while remaining a non-self-crossing path at finite steps.

Formula / model:
Iterative substitution of left/right turns in a folded strip sequence.
