Barnsley Fern
Domain: fractals | Category: Recursive & IFS

Principle:
- An iterated function system using four affine transformations.
- Randomly chosen transforms create a fern distribution through the chaos game.
- Probabilities control stem, leaflet, and self-similar sub-leaf density.

Formula / model:
x_{n+1}=A_i x_n+b_i with transform i selected by probability p_i.
