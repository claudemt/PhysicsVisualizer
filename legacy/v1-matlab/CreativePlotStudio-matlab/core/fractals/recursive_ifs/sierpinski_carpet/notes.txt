Sierpinski Carpet
Domain: fractals | Category: Recursive & IFS

Principle:
- Recursive removal of the central square from a 3x3 subdivision.
- The process repeats on the remaining eight squares.
- The limiting object has zero area but non-integer dimension.

Formula / model:
Dimension D=log(8)/log(3).
