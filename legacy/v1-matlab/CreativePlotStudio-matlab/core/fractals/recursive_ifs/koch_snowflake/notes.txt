Koch Snowflake
Domain: fractals | Category: Recursive & IFS

Principle:
- Each segment is replaced by four segments forming an equilateral bump.
- The perimeter grows without bound while the area approaches a finite value.
- It is one of the canonical self-similar fractal curves.

Formula / model:
Dimension D=log(4)/log(3).
