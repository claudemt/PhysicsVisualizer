Nova Cubic Basin
Domain: fractals | Category: Newton & Orbit Traps

Principle:
- Nova fractals are relaxed Newton iterations with an added step parameter.
- Colors encode which cubic root is reached and how fast convergence occurs.
- Changing relaxation creates spiral basin boundaries.

Formula / model:
z_{n+1}=z_n - α f(z_n)/f'(z_n), usually f(z)=z^3-1.
