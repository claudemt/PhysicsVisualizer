Newton Basin
Domain: fractals | Category: Newton & Orbit Traps

Principle:
- Newton fractals visualize convergence basins of Newton's root-finding method.
- Each pixel starts at a complex initial guess and converges to one root.
- Fractal basin boundaries show sensitivity of nonlinear iteration.

Formula / model:
z_{n+1}=z_n - f(z_n)/f'(z_n).
