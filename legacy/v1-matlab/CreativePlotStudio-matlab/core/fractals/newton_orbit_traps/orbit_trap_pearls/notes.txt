Orbit Trap Pearls
Domain: fractals | Category: Newton & Orbit Traps

Principle:
- Orbit-trap coloring records the closest approach to a geometric trap.
- This reveals internal paths of the orbit instead of only escape speed.
- Circular traps create pearl-like bands in Mandelbrot/Julia fields.

Formula / model:
Color ∝ min_n distance(z_n, trap).
