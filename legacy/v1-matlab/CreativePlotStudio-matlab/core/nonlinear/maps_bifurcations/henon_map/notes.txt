Henon Map
Domain: nonlinear | Category: Maps & Bifurcations

Principle:
- A classic 2D invertible map with stretching and folding.
- The attractor is fractal for standard parameter values.
- It is a discrete-time analogue of chaotic dissipative dynamics.

Formula / model:
x_{n+1}=1-a x_n^2+y_n, y_{n+1}=b x_n.
