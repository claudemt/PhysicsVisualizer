Circle Map Tongues
Domain: nonlinear | Category: Maps & Bifurcations

Principle:
- Models a periodically forced oscillator or rotator.
- Regions of rational rotation number form Arnold tongues.
- The diagram organizes synchronization and mode locking.

Formula / model:
