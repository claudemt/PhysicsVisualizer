Lyapunov Carpet
Domain: nonlinear | Category: Maps & Bifurcations

Principle:
- Uses Lyapunov exponents to classify local stability of a parameter-varying map.
- Positive exponent means exponential divergence of nearby trajectories.
- The resulting diagram is a nonlinear stability landscape.

Formula / model:
λ = average log of derivative magnitude along the orbit.
