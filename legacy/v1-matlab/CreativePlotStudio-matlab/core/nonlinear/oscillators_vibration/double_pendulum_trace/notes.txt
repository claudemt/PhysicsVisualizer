Double Pendulum Trace
Domain: nonlinear | Category: Oscillators & Vibration

Principle:
- A coupled nonlinear mechanical system with sensitive dependence on initial conditions.
- The trace of the lower mass can become chaotic.
- Energy exchange between arms creates complex trajectories.

Formula / model:
Two coupled second-order pendulum equations derived from the Lagrangian.
