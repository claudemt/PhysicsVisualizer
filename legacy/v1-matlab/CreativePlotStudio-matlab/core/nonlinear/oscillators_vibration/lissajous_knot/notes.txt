Lissajous Knot
Domain: nonlinear | Category: Oscillators & Vibration

Principle:
- A 3D parametric curve using sinusoidal coordinates with different frequencies.
- Frequency ratios determine knot closure and complexity.
- Phase offsets control the apparent weaving.

Formula / model:
x=sin(a t+δ), y=sin(b t), z=sin(c t+φ).
