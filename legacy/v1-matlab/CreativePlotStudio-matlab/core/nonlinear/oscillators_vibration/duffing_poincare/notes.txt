Duffing Poincare
Domain: nonlinear | Category: Oscillators & Vibration

Principle:
- A nonlinear forced oscillator with cubic stiffness.
- Sampling once per forcing period gives a Poincare section.
- The section reveals periodic, quasi-periodic, or chaotic response.

Formula / model:
x''+δx'+αx+βx^3=γ cos(ωt).
