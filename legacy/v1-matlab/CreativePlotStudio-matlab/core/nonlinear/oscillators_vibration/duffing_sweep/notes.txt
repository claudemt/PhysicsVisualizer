Duffing Sweep
Domain: nonlinear | Category: Oscillators & Vibration

Principle:
- Sweeps the forcing amplitude and samples the stroboscopic state.
- Branching indicates bifurcations and multistability.
- It is the nonlinear vibration analogue of a bifurcation diagram.

Formula / model:
Same Duffing equation, with γ varied across the horizontal axis.
