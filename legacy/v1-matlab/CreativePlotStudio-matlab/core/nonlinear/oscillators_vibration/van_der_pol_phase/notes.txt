Van der Pol Phase
Domain: nonlinear | Category: Oscillators & Vibration

Principle:
- A self-excited oscillator with nonlinear damping.
- Negative damping at small amplitude and positive damping at large amplitude create a limit cycle.
- The phase portrait shows attraction toward that cycle.

Formula / model:
x''-μ(1-x²)x'+x=0.
