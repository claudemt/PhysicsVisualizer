Chladni Resonance
Domain: nonlinear | Category: Oscillators & Vibration

Principle:
- Standing wave modes on a plate create nodal lines where vibration amplitude is zero.
- The plotted pattern resembles particles collecting on nodal curves.
- Mode numbers determine the symmetry of the resonance.
