De Jong Attractor
Domain: nonlinear | Category: Strange Attractors

Principle:
- A two-dimensional trigonometric map.
- Sine/cosine recurrence folds the plane into a bounded cloud.
- Fine point accumulation reveals the invariant measure of the map.

Formula / model:
x_{n+1}=sin(a y_n)-cos(b x_n), y_{n+1}=sin(c x_n)-cos(d y_n).
