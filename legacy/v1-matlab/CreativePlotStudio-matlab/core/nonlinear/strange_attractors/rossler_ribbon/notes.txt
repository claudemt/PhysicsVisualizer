Rossler Ribbon
Domain: nonlinear | Category: Strange Attractors

Principle:
- A continuous-time chaotic flow with spiral motion and slow folding.
- It is simpler than Lorenz but still exhibits chaos through a folded band.
- The attractor often appears as a twisted ribbon.

Formula / model:
dx/dt=-y-z, dy/dt=x+ay, dz/dt=b+z(x-c).
