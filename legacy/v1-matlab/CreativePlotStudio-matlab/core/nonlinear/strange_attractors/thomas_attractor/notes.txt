Thomas Attractor
Domain: nonlinear | Category: Strange Attractors

Principle:
- A cyclically symmetric system using sinusoidal feedback.
- The same damping is applied to all coordinates.
- For small damping, trajectories form a smooth chaotic tangle.

Formula / model:
dx/dt=sin(y)-b x, dy/dt=sin(z)-b y, dz/dt=sin(x)-b z.
