Clifford Attractor
Domain: nonlinear | Category: Strange Attractors

Principle:
- A 2D discrete map using sine and cosine of the previous point.
- Repeated iteration produces dense strange attractor point clouds.
- Parameters control the symmetry, lobes, and filament density.

Formula / model:
x_{n+1}=sin(a y_n)+c cos(a x_n), y_{n+1}=sin(b x_n)+d cos(b y_n).
