Hopalong Attractor
Domain: nonlinear | Category: Strange Attractors

Principle:
- A discrete map with a sign-dependent square-root nonlinearity.
- The branch structure creates shells and arcs.
- Despite a short formula, it can produce ornate attractor geometry.

Formula / model:
x_{n+1}=y_n-sign(x_n)sqrt(|b x_n-c|), y_{n+1}=a-x_n.
