Aizawa Attractor
Domain: nonlinear | Category: Strange Attractors

Principle:
- A 3D nonlinear flow with toroidal folding.
- Dissipation keeps the orbit bounded while nonlinear terms stretch the flow.
- The attractor appears like a folded vortex shell.

Formula / model:
Aizawa ODE with z-dependent rotation and cubic z damping.
