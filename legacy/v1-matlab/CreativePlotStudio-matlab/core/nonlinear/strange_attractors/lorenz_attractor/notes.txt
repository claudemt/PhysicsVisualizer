Lorenz Attractor
Domain: nonlinear | Category: Strange Attractors

Principle:
- A three-variable model of thermal convection.
- Trajectories stay bounded but never settle to a fixed cycle in chaotic regimes.
- The butterfly shape reflects stretching and folding in phase space.
