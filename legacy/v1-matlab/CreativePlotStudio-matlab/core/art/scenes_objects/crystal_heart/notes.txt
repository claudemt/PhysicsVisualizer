Crystal Heart
Domain: art | Category: Scenes & Objects

Principle:
- Uses a classical parametric heart curve as the base locus.
- At many heart-curve points, small random crystal segments are grown outward.
- The final scene combines a deterministic 2D heart with stochastic 3D crystal geometry.

Formula / model:
x=16 sin^3(t), y=13 cos(t)-5 cos(2t)-2 cos(3t)-cos(4t).
