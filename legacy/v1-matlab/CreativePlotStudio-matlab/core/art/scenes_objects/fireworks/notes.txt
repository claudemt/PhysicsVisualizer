Fireworks
Domain: art | Category: Scenes & Objects

Principle:
- Starts from random bright noise points, dilates them, then smears them with a wind-like decay.
- A polar transform converts horizontal streaks into radial explosion trails.
- A radial color map tints the grayscale explosion intensity.

Formula / model:
I_{k+1}(x,y)=max(I_k(x,y), ratio I_k(x-1,y)); then remap from Cartesian to polar.

Export note: rotating the preview before export preserves the current view.
