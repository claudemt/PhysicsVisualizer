Moonlit Mountains
Domain: art | Category: Scenes & Objects

Principle:
- Builds a layered 2D landscape using gradient sky, translucent clouds, and noise-generated mountain profiles.
- The mountain ridges use inverse FFT noise to create smooth natural height variation.
- A dark palette and alpha compositing make the mountain layers sit inside a night atmosphere.

Formula / model:
Mountain height uses abs(ifftn(exp(i random phase)/r^p)) sampled along a ridge row.

Export note: rotating the preview before export preserves the current view.
