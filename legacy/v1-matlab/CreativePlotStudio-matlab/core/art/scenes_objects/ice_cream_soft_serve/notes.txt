Ice Cream Soft Serve
Domain: art | Category: Scenes & Objects

Principle:
- Builds a soft-serve swirl surface using radius modulation along height and angle.
- Optional cone geometry is a tapered cylinder with grid-like cone lines.
- Flavor controls the material color, while lighting highlights the 3D surface ridges.

Formula / model:
R(theta,z)=base(z)+ridge sin(k theta + m z), then X=R cos theta, Y=R sin theta.
