Bitwise Fractal
Domain: art | Category: Pixel & Texture

Principle:
- Uses modular arithmetic on pixel coordinates: mod(X,Y) and mod(Y,X).
- Combines bitwise operators AND, XOR, and OR to generate RGB channels.
- The apparent self-similarity comes from integer remainder periodicity and binary masks rather than a classical iterated complex map.

Formula / model:
R = mod(bitand(mod(X,Y),mod(Y,X)),255), G = mod(bitxor(...),255), B = mod(bitor(...),255).
