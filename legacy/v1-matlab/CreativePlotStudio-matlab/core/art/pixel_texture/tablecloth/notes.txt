Tablecloth
Domain: art | Category: Pixel & Texture

Principle:
- Uses perspective-like scaling s = 3/(j+99) to compress rows with depth.
- Adds sinusoidal distortion to simulate cloth waviness.
- Different spatial frequencies are mapped to RGB channels to create woven colored checks.

Formula / model:
y = (j + 35 sin((i^2 + 5(j-700)^2)/(100N))) s, then parity patterns define color.
