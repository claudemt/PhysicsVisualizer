Music Score
Domain: art | Category: Pixel & Texture

Principle:
- Creates pseudo-score stripes through sine quantization and bitwise masking.
- The fixed/floor operation converts smooth sinusoidal variation into discrete staff-like bands.
- The result is abstract generative texture, not a semantic music transcription.

Formula / model:
P = mod(bitand(fix(100 sin(fix((i+400)(j+100)/11115))), i) * 1021, 256).
