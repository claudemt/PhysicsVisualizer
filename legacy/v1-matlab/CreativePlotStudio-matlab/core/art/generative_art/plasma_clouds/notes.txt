Plasma Clouds
Domain: art | Category: Generative Art

Principle:
- Creates smooth cloud-like fields using interpolated random values and color gradients.
- The visual effect is procedural texture rather than a physical fluid simulation.
- Color interpolation maps scalar intensity into a luminous plasma palette.

Formula / model:
A scalar noise field F(x,y) is normalized and rendered through a multi-stop colormap.

Export note: rotating the preview before export preserves the current view.
