Phyllotaxis Sunflower
Domain: art | Category: Generative Art

Principle:
- Uses Fermat spiral placement and the golden angle to mimic sunflower seed arrangements.
- Point radius scales with sqrt(n), giving approximately uniform packing density.
- The golden angle minimizes radial alignment and creates visible parastichy spirals.

Formula / model:
theta_n = n * 137.50776°, r_n = c sqrt(n).
