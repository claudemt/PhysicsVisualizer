Superformula Bloom
Domain: art | Category: Generative Art

Principle:
- Uses Johan Gielis' superformula to generate organic polar outlines.
- Changing exponents and symmetry parameter m produces petal-like or star-like forms.
- The project renders a layered bloom by sampling the formula over polar coordinates.

Formula / model:
r(φ)=((|cos(mφ/4)/a|)^n2 + (|sin(mφ/4)/b|)^n3)^(-1/n1).
