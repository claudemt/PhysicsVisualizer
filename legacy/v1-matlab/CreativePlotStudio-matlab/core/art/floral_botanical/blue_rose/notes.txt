Blue Rose
Domain: art | Category: Floral & Botanical

Principle:
- Uses a parametric surface in polar-like coordinates.
- A decaying angle p(t) controls petal opening from the center outward.
- The height and radius couple x and t to form layered rose petals.

Formula / model:
r = u(x sin p + y cos p), h = u(x cos p - y sin p), plotted as (r cos t, r sin t, h).
