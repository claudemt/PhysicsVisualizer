Rose Ball
Domain: art | Category: Floral & Botanical

Principle:
- Constructs a rose surface and replicates it with 3D rotation matrices.
- Rotations distribute the rose patches over directions similar to spherical packing.
- The color map is height-dependent, so each petal shares the same internal gradient.

Formula / model:
Rotated coordinates are [X',Y',Z']^T = R [X,Y,Z]^T with yaw and roll matrices.

Export note: rotating the preview before export preserves the current view.
