Sakura Tree
Domain: art | Category: Floral & Botanical

Principle:
- Uses an iterative branching system storing branch tip position and angle.
- At each generation, a random mask decides whether a branch splits or continues.
- Late-generation endpoints are plotted as blossoms, producing a stochastic tree silhouette.

Formula / model:
New tips are advanced by [cos(theta), sin(theta)]L and rotated by ±pi/10.
